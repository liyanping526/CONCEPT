Chinatown

New York Chinatown is located on the southern of Lower Manhattan in New York City. The area is centered on Mott Street, including Canal Street, Bayard Street, Pell Street, Lafayette Street, Bowery Street and East Broadway. Today, Chinatown has expanded to 45 streets, more than 4 square kilometers of an area in New York City. The change in Chinatown in New York City is a history of the growth and development of overseas Chinese.


One-third of the shops in Chinatown are Chinese restaurants. On both sides of the street mixed with piles of fruit, herbs, seafood stalls. For visitors who want to experience the unique atmosphere of Chinatown, get off at the Canal Street Subway Station, walk towards Mott Street. This way they can fully understand.
