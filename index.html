In the 1950s, there were 1,820 Puerto Ricans living in Sunset Park; by 1970, this number had grown to nearly 24,000. As with all new groups coming to Sunset Park, they settled along the waterfront, in residences vacated by their escalating predecessors. where they took up jobs in the industrials center of the neighborhood.
